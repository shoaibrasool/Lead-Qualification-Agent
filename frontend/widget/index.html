How it works

Three steps from visitor to qualified lead — fully automated.

1

Visitor starts a chat

A website visitor clicks the chat bubble and starts a natural conversation. The AI asks one question at a time to collect key info.

2

AI scores against your ICP

Our deterministic engine scores the lead across 6 dimensions — budget, authority, need, timeline, company fit, and engagement.

3

Auto-book or flag

High scores (≥70) get an auto-booked demo. Medium scores (40-69) flag for follow-up. Low scores (<40) get a polite decline. You get a Slack alert either way.
